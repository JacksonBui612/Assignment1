Summary and Features of project

+ Nowadays, with the increasing number of products in the market, it is very important to promote products and meet the users' needs. In Vietnam, there is a lot of e-commerce site including Shopee, Tiki, LazadaVn or FPT Shop where customers can buy things easily. However, the number of products is still limited. So my project is about an e-commerce site called '' FastGoods" which connects to every supermarket and seller in Vietnam to help customers find things they want to buy quickly without going anywhere.

+ More than millions of people buying things through e-commerce sites day by day because most of them do not have much time for shopping. Especially when people have to go to the supermarket every week to buy food and goods, they are more likely to consider it as a demand than a hobby. Therefore, FastGoods can be a good idea to solve this problem as it can use your location to recommend you some supermarket nearby and the users can collect the goods they want and pay by credit card or through PayPal. It would be much easier for anyone who wants to save time for shopping.

User-Friendly

This software interface is very simple which means that it is easy to use. The goal is to help shoppers get what they want faster and without running into unnecessary complexity.

Security Features

SSL Certificate, Two-factor authentication, using firewall and privacy policy link in footer are very crucial to protect customer’s information.

User-generated Reviews

The fact is that when buying things on the internet, users often read reviews before making a decision. Using reviews as user-generated content to drive social proof and build a large number of fans.

Mobile-Friendly Website

Provide users with a better experience, more friendly website, the amount of website visits will increase when many devices can access the website.

Special Offers

When the customers get special deals, it makes them want to buy more and spend more time searching for things.

High resolution photos and videos

Ecommerce website should display many photos per product in order to increase the effectiveness of advertising products and gain a lot of profit.

Wish Lists

The users can mark the products they want to buy in the future. It is also a chance to share the brand with the others. When sharing the wish list to the other people, it sends free traffic with built-in social proof to a website.

Find-in-stores

This feature will help the shopper check if the product they want to buy is available in local stores or not because shoppers tend to research online before purchasing in a store.

Related items

This will help buyers find more similar product categories and have more options while shopping.

Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

Frequently asked questions are also helpful when shopping online. Provide a help area to resolve common customer issues. Buyers often ask about product support, returns, lost account or passwords.

Advanced Payment Option

Nowadays, there are many payment options like Paypal or Apple Pay. However, in order to buy things, buyers have to register an account so that the system can easily manage your shopping cart.

Detailed shipping information

It is very crucial that the shipping information should be included in the checkout process and the delivery time should be also added as well. It will motivate people to buy more things if they get a discount or free shipping cost.
